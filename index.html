About Me

Hello! I'm Bhanuka, a Video Editor based in Colombo.

My journey into video editing began with a fascination for how a story can be shaped in post-production. The right cut, the perfect color grade, or a powerful sound effect can completely transform a collection of clips into an emotional and impactful experience.

I specialize in creating dynamic content, from high-energy social media ads and cinematic car edits to compelling corporate stories. I am proficient with the Adobe Creative Suite and DaVinci Resolve, and I thrive on collaborating with clients to bring their vision to life.
